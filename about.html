About Me

I am currently living in Helsingborg, Sweden, studying Grundläggande Programmering. Prior to that I spent a couple of seasons working abroad for the travel company TUI UK, before which I studied History at the University of Reading. I grew up using computers on a daily basis and I'm now hoping to be able to develop my skills and begin a career in computer programming. In order to achieve that goal, I have enrolled on a course for Grundläggande Programmering at Komvux Helsingborg and in C# at Malmo University in Autumn 2020. I have also studied Harvard University's CS50x course, where I have developed my understanding of computer science, and have learnt how to write programs in C, ranging from a simple text encryption program to a program which applies filters to images, and even programs to determine the outcome of elections.

Outside of my work and studies, I'm a very energetic person and have a passion for squash, snowboarding and all things travel. I've been lucky enough to spend two seasons abroad working for TUI - one season in the French Alps and one in Crete. I've also spent two months backpacking around South America in 2015 and a month interrailing around Europe in 2016. If you'd like to learn a little bit more about me, or simply say hi, feel free to get in touch.
